Graphs

Here we see three different graphs. The first graph represents the data from the HVAC zones, the second represents data from the proximity zones, and the third is a parallel coordinates graph representing the amount of times each employee is spotted by the proximity sensors. When pressed on a line (an employee) a heatmap of the zones is depicted next to the parallel coordinates graph that shows how many times that specific employee has been spotted in each zone on the chosen floor.
