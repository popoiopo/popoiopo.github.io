Findings of this challenge

Here we discuss all the anomalies of the dataset.

Results

Results from these linegraphs are discussed here. When looking at the linegraphs and comparing the readings per floor or zone, patterns and anomalies can be found. These can be used to analyze the behavior of the employees at GAStech and to evaluate the risks. They can also be used to spot anomalies and irregularities throughout the two-week period. Finally we can relate the patterns to the anomalies and try to explain the causes using the behavior of the employees.
